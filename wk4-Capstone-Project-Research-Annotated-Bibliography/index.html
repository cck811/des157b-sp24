This article from Pew Research Center dives deep into the issues of wealth inequality in America, focusing on how it looks and why it's happening. It's super useful for my project because it provides hard data and expert analysis on the distribution of wealth which can help back up any claims I make. The World Bank’s report on global wealth inequality gives a broad look at how wealth is spread out across different countries and what that means for everyone. Brookings Institution's article tackles how tax policy impacts economic inequality in the U.S. This is spot-on for my project because it explores the direct effects of fiscal decisions on wealth gaps.
